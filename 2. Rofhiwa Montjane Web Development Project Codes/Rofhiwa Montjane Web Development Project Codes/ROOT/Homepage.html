Welcome to
TacticsInTechIT


TacticsInTechIT is an IT troubleshooting organization that provides affordable and accessible solutions for technological house appliances and ICT assets. Our mission is to empower rural communities with the knowledge and skills to maintain and repair their own devices, and to reduce electronic waste and pollution. Our vision is to create a network of IT experts and volunteers who can offer online and offline support to anyone in need.






Our goals are to:

  • Provide free or low-cost IT education and training to rural areas.
  • Offer online and offline IT troubleshooting and repair services to rural areas.
  • Collect and recycle electronic waste from rural areas.
  • Promote IT literacy and awareness among rural communities.


We aim to address the problems of unemployment, poverty, digital divide, and environmental degradation that affect many rural areas in South Africa. We believe that by equipping rural people with IT skills and tools, we can help them improve their livelihoods, access information and opportunities, and protect the environment.
