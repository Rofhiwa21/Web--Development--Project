Our Services

At TacticsInTechIT, we offer a range of IT troubleshooting and repair services to suit your needs and budget. Whether you have a problem with your household appliance or your ICT asset, we have the solution for you.


Our services include:

Household Appliance Troubleshooting

If you have a faulty or malfunctioning household appliance, such as a fridge, microwave, or television, we can help you diagnose and fix the problem. We can provide online guidance, offline assistance, or on-site visit depending on the situation.


Our benefits are:

  • We can save you time and money by avoiding unnecessary replacements or purchases.
  • We can extend the lifespan and performance of your household appliance.
  • We can prevent potential hazards or damages caused by faulty appliances.


ICT Asset Repair

If you have a broken or damaged ICT asset, such as a phone, laptop, or computer, we can help you restore and upgrade it. We can provide online support, offline service, or on-site intervention depending on the issue.


Our benefits are:

  • We can recover your data and files from your ICT asset.
  • We can improve the functionality and security of your ICT asset.
  • We can enhance the features and capabilities of your ICT asset.

Our Environmental Responsibility

At TacticsInTechIT, we care about the environment and the impact of our IT activities on it. We are aware that electronic waste, or e-waste, is a growing problem that poses serious threats to human health and natural resources. E-waste is any discarded electronic device or component that contains hazardous materials, such as lead, mercury, cadmium, and brominated flame retardants. E-waste can contaminate soil, water, and air, and cause various diseases and disorders. According to the [Global E-waste Monitor 2020], South Africa generated 2.2 million tonnes of e-waste in 2019, ranking 25th in the world. Only 12% of this e-waste was collected and recycled. The rest was dumped in landfills or burned in informal settings. This is unacceptable and unsustainable.


We follow these principles:

  • We reduce e-waste by repairing and reusing IT devices whenever possible.
  • We collect and recycle e-waste from our clients and partners using certified e-waste recyclers.
  • We educate our clients and partners on the importance and benefits of e-waste management.

That is why we are committed to responsible e-waste disposal and recycling.

We also invite you to join us in our efforts to protect the environment from e-waste.

You can do so by:

  • Donating your old or unwanted IT devices to us for recycling.
  • Participating in our local e-waste cleanup events.
  • Spreading the word about e-waste issues and solutions.

Our Community Engagement

At TacticsInTechIT, we are more than just an IT troubleshooting organization. We are also a community of IT enthusiasts, learners, and leaders who want to make a positive difference in the world. We are actively involved in various community initiatives, partnerships, and success stories that showcase our impact and values.


Some of our community engagement activities are:

Community Initiatives

We organize and participate in various community initiatives that aim to promote IT literacy, awareness, and empowerment among rural communities.


Some of our initiatives are:

  • IT Day: We host an annual event where we invite local schools, businesses, and organizations to learn about IT and its applications. We offer demonstrations, exhibitions, competitions, and prizes to encourage participation and engagement.
  • IT Club: We sponsor and mentor an after-school club for students who are interested in IT and want to learn more. We provide them with IT resources, projects, and opportunities to develop their IT skills and passions.
  • IT Challenge: We launch a monthly challenge where we invite anyone to submit their IT ideas or solutions for a specific problem or theme. We select and reward the best entries and showcase them on our website and social media.

Community Partnerships

We collaborate and partner with various community stakeholders who share our vision and mission of creating a more inclusive and sustainable IT society.


Some of our partners are:

  • Rural Development Agency (RDA): We work with RDA to identify and support rural communities that need IT assistance and education. We also help RDA to monitor and evaluate the impact of our IT services on rural development.
  • South African E-waste Alliance (SAEWA): We join SAEWA to advocate for responsible e-waste management and recycling in South Africa. We also contribute to SAEWA's research and policy recommendations on e-waste issues and solutions.
  • IT Academy: We partner with IT Academy to provide our trainees with accredited IT courses and certifications. We also refer our trainees to IT Academy for further education and career opportunities.
