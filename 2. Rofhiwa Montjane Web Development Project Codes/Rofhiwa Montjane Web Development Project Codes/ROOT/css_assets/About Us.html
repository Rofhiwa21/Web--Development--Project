About Us

TacticsInTechIT is an IT troubleshooting organization that was founded in 2023 by a group of passionate IT professionals and volunteers who wanted to make a difference in rural communities.

Our founders are:

Rofhiwa Montjane

A software engineer and web developer who has over 10 years of experience in the IT industry. He is the CEO and lead trainer of TacticsInTechIT.

Ntombi Dlamini

A computer scientist and data analyst who has a PhD in artificial intelligence. She is the CTO and lead researcher of TacticsInTechIT.

Tharollo Makgatho

A hardware engineer and technician who has a diploma in electronics. He is the COO and lead repairer of TacticsInTechIT.

Our core values are:

  1. Service: We are committed to providing high-quality and affordable IT solutions to our clients and partners.
  2. Education: We are dedicated to empowering our clients and partners with the knowledge and skills to use and maintain their IT devices.
  3. Innovation: We are constantly exploring new ways to improve our IT services and products, and to adapt to the changing needs of our clients and partners.
  4. Sustainability: We are mindful of the environmental impact of our IT activities, and we strive to reduce, reuse, and recycle our electronic waste.
  5. Community: We are proud to be part of the rural communities we serve, and we seek to build long-term relationships based on trust and respect.

We believe that by sharing our IT expertise and resources with rural communities, we can help them overcome the challenges they face, such as unemployment, poverty, digital divide, and environmental degradation. We hope that by doing so, we can also inspire more people to join us in our mission to create a more inclusive and sustainable IT society.
